07

Venue-Link Theming Lock

venue-link is a public, un-branded booking surface a venue drops into their own site. it runs on this system but strips anything that would read as A-List the app. these rules are non-negotiable on venue-link.

  • ●powered-by footer, always. "POWERED BY A-LIST" set at 8px · tracking .4em · white/20. ambient, never a link, never larger.
  • ○no neon. the logo gradient does not appear on venue-link — not in a mark, not in a push. platinum and white opacity only.
  • ○no A-List branding. no app icon, no "get the app" before confirmation, no signup wall ahead of checkout. the venue is the host; A-List is the rail underneath.
  • ●platinum stays reserved. hero & confirmation only — same rule as everywhere, enforced harder here where the venue's name leads.
